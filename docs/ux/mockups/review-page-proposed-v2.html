What's trimmed vs v1 mockup

  • 1No category tagging at all — 诊断 / 检查 / 治疗 / 随访 labels dropped. Doctor reads the title. One less visual element per card.
  • 2Citations as a clickable chip with human-readable name — 依据：溃疡鉴别要点 ›, not the internal [KB-N]. Tap to open the referenced rule.
  • 3Pending card = title + body + actions. No category row, no chip row.
  • 4`+ 添加我的建议` moved to NavBar ···. Rare action, out of the main flow.
  • 5Accepted cards collapse to one line with light-green bg — ✓ + title, full light-green fill. Tap to reopen. Saves ~70px per accepted item AND makes accepted state scannable at a glance.
