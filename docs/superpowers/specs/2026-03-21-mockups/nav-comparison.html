Patient Portal Navigation: Competitor Patterns

Research from 好大夫, 微医, MyChart, and Chinese AI pre-consultation platforms

Competitor Insights

好大夫在线
Before/during/after consultation model. Post-visit: prescription viewing, medication diary, medical history timeline, disease diary for tracking changes
微医 WeDoctor
Feature-centric tabs: appointments, consultations, prescriptions, medical records. Many entry points, marketplace model
MyChart (Epic)
Comprehensive but cluttered — 67% of users said "too many options". Top priorities: Visits, Health Summary, Test Results
Chinese AI Platforms
WeChat mini-programs: dedicated doctor binding, auto-archived records, chronic disease follow-up with health risk prediction

My Recommendation: B (Visit Lifecycle)

Why: Our product is doctor-owned and visit-oriented (not a marketplace like 好大夫/微医). The patient's core question after a visit is "what do I need to do?" — not "where are my records?" Three tabs matches WeChat's simplicity. MyChart proved that feature-centric navigation overwhelms patients. The Home tab surfaces the latest visit with actionable next steps, which is exactly the post-visit value we're building. For new patients (pre-first-visit), the Home tab naturally becomes the interview entry point.
