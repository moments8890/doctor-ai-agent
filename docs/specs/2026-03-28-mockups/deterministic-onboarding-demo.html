Product Mock

Deterministic Discovery for Solo Doctors

This mock turns the current dashboard into a guided first-run path. The doctor is not asked to guess where to start. The app explicitly conducts them from teach the AI to diagnosis proof, reply proof, patient intake preview, and finally generated tasks.

Mission 1 · 教 AI 一条规则 Start from URL, file/image, or text. Save one rule, then immediately see where it will be used through deterministic examples.
Mission 2 · 体验患者预问诊 Preview the patient path directly from QR or doctor chat, then bridge back to doctor review and generated follow-up tasks.
